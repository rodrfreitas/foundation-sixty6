Who we are

We are the parents and supporters of an individual who has faced significant barriers in accessing adequate mental healthcare for severe conditions recently diagnosed under the umbrella of psychosis.

About

Foundation Sixty6 is a collaboration of parents and supporters who have recently encountered personal difficulties in obtaining adequate access to mental healthcare for severe conditions. Observing the widespread challenges many face in accessing these resources, we are dedicated to addressing the barriers and alleviating the suffering experienced by those in need.

Our foundation was created aiming to advocate tirelessly for these people and their families who suffer from severe mental health conditions. We strongly believe in hope, resilience, and the power of collective compassion, together, we can strive to create a world where mental health is understood, accessible, and embraced for and by everyone.
