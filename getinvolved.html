Calling All Healthcare Providers

Join Us in Making a Difference

Healthcare Providers play a crucial role in our mission, offering specialized care and guidance to help individuals navigate the complexities of psychosis-related mental health issues. By partnering with us, healthcare professionals can extend their reach and make a tangible difference in the lives of young people in need.

By joining us, you'll have the opportunity to directly contribute to our mission of empowering youth through specialized care and guidance. Together, we can create a network of support that ensures no young person faces mental health issues alone. Join our team of compassionate healthcare providers and be a part of the solution.

Together, we can make a real difference in the lives of individuals struggling with mental health challenges.

Empower Talent

Partner with Foundation Sixty6

Calling all employers! Foundation Sixty6 offers a unique opportunity to partner with us in supporting talented individuals who receive assistance from our foundation. By collaborating with us, you can empower these individuals to shine in the workplace and contribute their skills to your team.

Our foundation identifies and nurtures exceptional talent among those facing mental health challenges related to psychosis. Through our support network, we provide resources and guidance to help individuals succeed professionally.

As an employer, you can make a meaningful impact by offering inclusive employment opportunities. Partner with Foundation Sixty6 to create a more diverse and inclusive workplace while tapping into a pool of talented individuals eager to contribute.

Join us in breaking down barriers and creating pathways to success for individuals facing mental health challenges. Partner with Foundation Sixty6 and empower talent in our community.
